ELDER DRAGON ADVISORY

Extreme Weather in the New World


Sightings of an extremely dangerous specimen of Teostra have been reported in the Wildspire Waste.


According to reports, this specific Teostra possesses a more powerful blast powder,
which has caused temperatures to rise drastically in the Wildspire Waste and its neighboring locales.
Hunters are strongly advised to bring Cool Drinks and Fireproof Mantles on their hunts for the time being.

Moreover, a special quest to slay the Teostra has been posted. Hunters may approach their Handlers
or talk to the Gathering Hub Lass in order to take this quest.


Stay hydrated, Hunters.
May the Sapphire Star light our way.
