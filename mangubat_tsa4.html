EXTREME WEATHER ADVISORY

Following the fall of the belligerent Teostra, torrential rainfall has been observed in numerous regions of the New World. This weather has greatly reduced our Hunters' vision, leading to injuries minor and serious alike.

This weather is speculated to have been caused by a special specimen of Namielle, which is reported to have a greater influence over the New World weather. Ecosystems across the New World are being severely affected, causing floods and ruining the local wildlife.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In line with this situation, the Commander has posted a special assignment to all dauntless Hunters willing to take down this new threat and restore balance to the ecosystem. As always, you can approach your Handlers to accept the assignment.

Stay safe, Hunters. May the Sapphire Star light our way.
